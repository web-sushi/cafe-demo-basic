Our Story

It all started with a simple question:
What if coffee could be more than just caffeine?

In the spring of 2019, Maria and James found themselves in the same place many of us do, burnt out, overworked, and desperately missing the simple joys of life. Over lukewarm coffee in a generic chain cafe, they began dreaming out loud. What would their perfect coffee shop look like? What would it feel like? More importantly, what would it mean to their community?

Six months later, armed with savings, support from family, and a whole lot of naive optimism, they signed the lease on a small corner space that had been everything from a barbershop to a bookstore over its hundred-year history. The bones were good, but everything else needed work. Friends pitched in on weekends, sanding floors and painting walls in exchange for the promise of free coffee for life, a promise we still honor today.

Today, Espresso Haven is more than a café. It's a gathering place where students find quiet corners to study, where friends reconnect over perfectly brewed lattes, and where neighbors become family. We source our beans directly from small farms, ensuring every cup supports sustainable practices and fair wages. Our pastries are baked fresh each morning, and our baristas are trained not just in technique, but in the art of making you feel welcome.

We believe that great coffee is about connection to the land, to the people who grow it, and to each other. Come visit us, and let's share a cup together.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes! We accept reservations for groups of 4 or more. For smaller groups, we operate on a first-come, first-served basis. You can make a reservation through our contact form or by calling us directly.

Do you have Wi-Fi?

Absolutely! We offer free high-speed Wi-Fi for all our guests. It's perfect for remote work or studying. We just ask that you be mindful of others during busy hours.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we're happy to accommodate! We offer vegan options, gluten-free pastries (limited selection), and alternative milks (oat, almond, soy). Please let us know about any allergies or dietary needs when you order.

Do you sell coffee beans?

Yes! We sell our house blend and seasonal selections by the bag. You can purchase them in-store or ask about our subscription service for regular deliveries.

Is the café child-friendly?

Very much so! We welcome families and have high chairs available. We also offer kid-friendly drinks like hot chocolate and steamed milk. The atmosphere is relaxed and perfect for families.

Do you host events or private parties?

We occasionally host small events like coffee tastings and book clubs. For private parties, please contact us at least two weeks in advance. We can accommodate groups up to 20 people.
