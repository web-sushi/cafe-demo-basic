Our Story : Where Every Cup Has a Story

It all started with a question:
What if coffee could be more than just caffeine?

In the spring of 2019, Maria and James found themselves in the same place many of us do, burnt out, overworked, and desperately missing the simple joys of life. Over lukewarm coffee in a generic chain cafe, they began dreaming out loud. What would their perfect coffee shop look like? What would it feel like? More importantly, what would it mean to their community?

Six months later, armed with savings, support from family, and a whole lot of naive optimism, they signed the lease on a small corner space that had been everything from a barbershop to a bookstore over its hundred-year history. The bones were good, but everything else needed work. Friends pitched in on weekends, sanding floors and painting walls in exchange for the promise of free coffee for life, a promise we still honor today.
